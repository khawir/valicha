About us

Valicha! is a large, modern 6 mtr Toowoomba based food trailer selling a range of incredible Peruvian fusion foods, Sweets and drinks.

Owned and Operated by Melisa Valencia (Executive Chef) and Phill Routledge (Hired muscle and chief dishwasher, But mostly the person ever encouraging Melisa to develop more her gift of cooking), Valicha started life from very humble beginnings around mid 2021 at a private BBQ at our home.

It was at this BBQ that Melisa prepared the most incredible Peruvian BBQ chicken on her small mini patio rotisserie for the first time since we had been together and I was blown away by the intense flavours all the way through the meat to the bones! That recipe was called Pollo la Brasa and it alone started our journey.

In a discussion between myself and our friends with Melisa we slowly started to encourage her to 'back herself in' and take it to the markets on Saturdays and Sundays because I knew people would love this charcoal chicken!. Over the next few months the idea sprouted and Melisa slowly found the confidence to give it a go...

Quickly a few new 3mtr gazebo's were sourced along with some basic cooking equipment. Along with a few things borrowed from a friend, a large banner print with the business name and logo on it was placed onto the main gazebo and wullah! - "Peru Chicken" (our business's first namesake) was born and at our first market at the PCYC in Toowoomba! 18 months later and we were fortunate enough, to purchase our beautiful new food trailer, we aptly named the good kitchen 'Valicha' - The nickname Mel's recently passed mother gave her.

Peruvian food is often referred to as Criolla or Creole food because of the incredible blend of Spanish, Asian, Incan Indian and other European cuisine influences which are integrated and very finely balanced into the Peruvian cooking culture.

Valicha specializes in balancing these incredible recipe's for the Australian palette, taking you and your taste buds on a food adventure back to the ancient Inca civilization’s that once flourished and ruled the incredible country of Peru!

Not being just another ‘Fast Food’ vendor flipping the ‘normal fare’, Valicha aim to give you something different, because, we’re not just about our awesome foods, in fact we hope to give you a little insight into the ancient Inca Indian and modern day Peruvian culture’s that we hope you will enjoy every bit as much as the foods we serve you!

Sincerely,

Mel and Phill.
